who is the modest reader?

I thought "The Modest Reader" was a cute title for a reading/blogger website. The definition of modest is 1) unassuming or moderate in the estimaion of one's abilities or achievements, 2) (of an amount, rate, or level) relatively moderate, limited, or small. I thought it would fit well with me personally, as I only read a few books a year. Outside of reading, I'm a graphic designer turned web developer. I'm currently earning a degree in Computer Science from Oregon State University. I have a cute dog name Bella, who is spoiled rotten. (Not by me, I adopted her that way). Once I finish my degree, I hope to find a job as a full stack developer, and build mobile games on the side as a hobby.
